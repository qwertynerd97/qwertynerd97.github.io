River Center Mobile

Access this Project

River Center Mobile creates an interactive, mobile experience for visitors to the Loxahatchee River Center. They can listen as an audio tour walks them through each of the exhibits in the Loxahatchee River Center, and learn more about the environment of the river, as well as engage their minds with an interactive scavenger hunt that tests their knowledge of each exhibit. Furthermore, the app includes a detailed walkthrough of the outdoor butterfly garden, with information about each of Florida's native plants.
